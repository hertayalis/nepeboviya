    Biography:

    Cote de Pablo was born on November 12, 1979 in Santiago, Chile, although she was raised in Miami, Florida. She studied musical theatre and graduated in 2000.

    In 2001, she appeared on the New York City Public Theatre Stage in the Shakespearean play “Measure for Measure”. She then moved on to roles on television such as in the series All My Children.

    In 2005, she landed the role of Ziva David in the CBS television series NCIS. She won an ALMA Award for her role in 2011 and NCIS became the most watched television program in America during its tenth season.
