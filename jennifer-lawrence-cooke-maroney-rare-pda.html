    Jennifer Lawrence and Fianc Cooke Maroney Rare PDA Is Giving Us All The Feels

    • We also got another view of her gorgeous Alison Lou engagement ring.
    • Page Six initially reported that the pair became engaged on February 5. The next day, an inside source close to J-Law and Maroney confirmed to BAZAAR.com that the reports are true.

    Jennifer Lawrence and fiancé Cooke Maroney were spotted getting cozy as they walked down the streets of New York City. Lawrence put her hand around Maroney's arm as the newly engaged couple explored the bustling city together.

    The Hunger Games and Red Sparrow star looked comfortable in an oversized cream coat over what appeared to be a white crewneck T-shirt, black skinny jeans, and a pair of cream pointy mules. Maroney looked equally laid-back yet stylish in a navy jacket over a green T-shirt, dark wash pants, and a pair of blue and white sneakers.

    In the adorable photograph, we got another look at the actress's stunning engagement ring. An insider confirmed to People that J-Law's ring "features an emerald-cut stone that sits on a thin gold band" by Manhattan-based fine jeweler Alison Lou.

    Splash News

    Our first glimpse of her ring occurred during Paris Fashion Week in February. While attending a runway show for Dior, Lawrence (who is a brand ambassador for the French fashion house) posed with her gorgeous piece of jewelry that complemented her chic grey outfit.

    Understandably, the extremely low-profile couple are keeping details of their upcoming wedding ceremony closely guarded. Page Six first reported the news of the couple's engagement two months ago. The following day, an inside source close to both J-Law and Maroney confirmed to BAZAAR.com that the engagement news is true.
