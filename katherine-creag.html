    Katherine Creag was an Emmy-winning Filipino-American television journalist who served as a news reporter for WNBC television in New York City. But she is best known for NBC's work on Today in New York and as well as WNYW's work on Good Day New York. 

    Katherine was born on 26 December 1973 in New York. She is the daughter of Valentino Creag (father) and the late Cecilia Creag (mother). Further, she studied at New York University which is located in New York City, New York. So far, she holds American citizenship and is of Asian ethnicity.

    Katherine Is No More In This World

    The TV personality Katherine who was grabbing wider media attention from her incredible journalism work is no more in this world. 

    The woman died on February 10, 2021, in Manhattan, New York. At the time of the death, Creag was 47 years old. 

    The exact reason behind Katherine's death is unknown but as per some sources, the reason behind her demise was the sudden "cardiopulmonary condition." Many of Creag's fans, friends, and well-wishers light up with tribute after her death.
