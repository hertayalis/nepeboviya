      Sam Faiers

      Fans of saw love bloom between castmates Joey and Sam and the couple had an on-off relationship for years, starting in 2010. The couple seemed to be forever after getting back together in 2012 with Joey proposing the following year, however their engagement was called off the same year and it appears things have gone south for the former lovebirds. In 2019, the star revealed that the pair hadn't spoken for several years.

      Amy Willerton

      In 2013, following his split from Sam, Joey took part in the 13th series of where he met model and former Miss Universe Amy. Joey got candid about their relationship in his autobiography, , however the pair weren't to last and ended up going their separate ways.

      Charlotte Stuchfield

      Joey began dating Charlotte in 2015 and the pair were spotted on romantic trips together, however the duo eventually went their separate ways.

      4/10

      Stephanie Pratt

      On one of Joey's first ventures into the reality dating scene, he ended up linking with star Stephanie Pratt, with the pair sharing some steamy scenes in a swimming pool. The pair dated for three months before calling things off and Stephanie has since said that the former couple are no longer on speaking terms.

      5/10

      Georgie Purves

      Joey dated hairstylist Georgie for a few months in 2017, with the couple going public at the launch of the star's clothing line, Fusey. However, the duo went their separate ways later in the year.

      6/10

      Ellie Brown

      Although this is Joey's first time on , he previously dated castmember Ellie Brown, who had appeared on the show's fourth series. The couple went official with a passionate kiss on Instagram, however, they weren't to last, even though they would later cross paths on .

      7/10

      Lorena Medina

      During his stint on , Joey hit it off with Brazilian model Lorena and the pair even stayed together after the conclusion of the show, ultimately moving in together. However, the pair broke things off in 2020, after Joey spent a night at Rita Ora's mansion with friends following a night on the town.

      At the time Medina said: "I knew Joey had spent the night out because I had talked to him when he was in the cab on the way back to Essex. He told me, 'I had a crazy night and I stayed at my friend's' and that was fine. But he didn't tell me it was a girl and he didn't mention Rita Ora."

      Brenda Santos

      After his relationship with Lorena came to an end, Joey found love with model Brenda Santos. The pair were together during the coronavirus lockdown, but alas, it wasn't meant to be and the pair called it quits after five months of dating.

      9/10

      Vanessa Bauer

      During his stint on , Joey was linked with his professional partner Vanessa Bauer, and although the couple wouldn't lift the lid on their bond during their time on the show, they did go official following the conclusion. Speaking to , Vanessa commented: "Me and Joey, we're doing very good – we are doing really good at the moment."
